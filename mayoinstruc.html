Instuctions

In a blender combine the 4 egg yolks, vinager, salt sugar, mustard powder and blend until well combined. Next put the blender on high, and start slowing pouring the oil into the blender. Once all the oil has been added, blend for a little bit longer to make sure that it is fully combined. Once done transfer into a container and store in the refigerator.
